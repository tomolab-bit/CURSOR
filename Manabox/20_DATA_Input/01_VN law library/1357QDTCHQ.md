DECISION

ON THE PROMULGATION OF THE CODE TABLE OF EXPORT AND IMPORT TYPES AND INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE

DIRECTOR GENERAL OF CUSTOMS

Pursuant to the Customs Law No. 54/2014/QH13 dated June 23, 2014;

Pursuant to Decree No. 08/2015/ND-CP dated January 21, 2015 of the Government detailing and providing measures for implementing the Customs Law on customs procedures, inspection, supervision and control; Decree No. 59/2018/ND-CP dated April 20, 2018 of the Government amending and supplementing a number of Articles of Decree No. 08/2015/ND-CP ;

Pursuant to Decision No. 65/2015/QD-TTg dated December 17, 2015 of the Prime Minister on defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the General Department of Customs under the Ministry of Finance;

Pursuant to Circular No. 38/2015/TT-BTC dated March 25, 2015 of the Minister of Finance regulating customs procedures; customs inspection and supervision; export tax, import tax and tax administration for exported and imported goods; Circular No. 39/2018/TT-BTC dated April 20, 2018 of the Minister of Finance amending and supplementing a number of Articles of Circular No. 38/2015/TT-BTC .

At the request of the Director of the Customs Supervision and Management Department,

DECISION:

Article 1. Promulgated together with this Decision is the Code Table of export and import types and instructions for use.

Article 2. Entry into force

This Decision takes effect from June 1, 2021 and replaces Official Dispatch No. 2765/TCHQ-GSQL dated April 1, 2015 of the General Department of Customs on import-export type codes on the VNACCS system.

Article 3. Transitional provisions

1. For customs declarations registered under the corresponding type before the effective date of this Decision, if there is a change in the purpose of use or registration of customs declaration under a new type of export or import, the type code shall be used according to the provisions of this Decision from the time of registration of the new customs declaration.

2. For the type codes that have been prescribed in this Decision but the customs procedures have not been specifically prescribed in the Government's Decrees and Circulars of the Ministry of Finance, they shall not be implemented until there are specific regulations.

Article 4. Implementation:

1. Heads of units under and directly under the General Department of Customs are responsible for implementing this Decision.

2. The Customs Departments of provinces and cities shall direct their subordinate Customs Sub-Departments to publicly announce this Decision at customs clearance locations so that customs declarants can know and implement it.

3. During the implementation process, if any problems arise, the Customs Departments of provinces and cities shall synthesize and report to the General Department of Customs (through the Customs Management and Supervision Department) for implementation instructions./.



Recipients:
- As per Article 4;
- Vietnam Chamber of Commerce and Industry (for coordination);
- Customs website (for notification);
- File: VT, GSQL (10 b).

Used in case Vietnamese enterprises import raw materials, supplies, machinery and equipment for domestic production (including imported goods to implement investment projects):

Used in case the enterprise transfers domestic consumption originating from temporary imports. In case of transferring domestic consumption from other sources, use code A42.

Used in the case of re-importing exported goods of all types of business export, export of processed products, export of manufactured products for export, export of products of export processing enterprises, export of raw materials ordered for processing abroad (including: raw materials, surplus supplies; scrap, waste products), other exported goods under type H21 but returned for domestic consumption or destruction.


5

A41


Import business of enterprises exercising import rights

Used in cases where foreign-invested enterprises (including export processing enterprises) and foreign enterprises not present in Vietnam import goods under import rights for direct sale in Vietnam (without production).


6

A42


Change of purpose of use or transfer of domestic consumption from other types, except temporary import

Use in case of:

a) Enterprises importing goods that are not subject to tax, are exempt from tax, or have tax incentives (including tariff quotas), and then change the purpose of use or transfer to domestic consumption, except for temporarily imported goods for re-export (including temporary import and re-export business).

b) Enterprises exporting goods on the spot exceed 15 days from the date of customs clearance of the on-spot export declaration, but the enterprise does not notify the customs authority that the corresponding on-spot import customs declaration has completed customs procedures.

19

G13


Duty-free temporary import

Use in case of:

a) Temporarily imported goods to organize or participate in fairs, exhibitions, product introductions, sports, cultural, artistic events or other events;

b) Temporarily imported machinery and equipment for testing and product research and development;

c) Machinery, equipment, and professional tools temporarily imported to serve work within a certain period of time or to serve processing for foreign traders or processing for export processing enterprises, except for machinery, equipment, tools, and means of transport of organizations and individuals that are allowed to temporarily import to carry out investment projects, construction, installation of works, and to serve production;

c) Machinery, equipment, components and spare parts temporarily imported to replace and repair foreign ships and aircraft;

d) Temporarily imported goods to supply foreign ships and aircraft anchored at Vietnamese ports;

d) Temporarily imported goods for warranty, repair and replacement;

e) Temporary import rotating vehicles to contain export and import goods;

g) Re-imported goods of all types of business exports, processed products, manufactured products for export, products of export processing enterprises, and other exported goods that are returned for repair, recycling, and then re-exported to customers or re-exported to third countries or to duty-free zones.

h) Machinery and equipment transferred from one processing contract to another

Enterprises must declare the type code along with tax-exempt or non-taxable codes depending on the above cases (refer to the Code Tables at www.customs.gov.vn)

20

b) Used in other temporary import cases not detailed in G11, G12, G13.

d) Temporarily exported goods of individuals exempted from tax by the Vietnamese government;

e) Temporarily exported goods are tools, tools, and working means temporarily exported for a limited period of time by agencies, organizations, or people leaving the country;

g) Temporarily exported goods are equipment, machinery, construction vehicles, molds, and samples for production, construction of projects, and testing;

h) Re-import of other temporarily exported goods.
