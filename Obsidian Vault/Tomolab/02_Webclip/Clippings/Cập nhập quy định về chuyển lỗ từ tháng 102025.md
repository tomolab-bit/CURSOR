## 1\. Update regulations on loss transfer from October 2025

Pursuant to Article 16 [of the Law on Corporate Income Tax 2025](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Luat-Thue-thu-nhap-doanh-nghiep-2025-so-67-2025-QH15-580594.aspx?anchor=dieu_16) (effective from October 1, 2025), provisions on loss carryover are as follows:

> ***Article 16. Loss transfer -*** [***Law on Corporate Income Tax 2025***](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Luat-Thue-thu-nhap-doanh-nghiep-2025-so-67-2025-QH15-580594.aspx?anchor=dieu_16)
> 
> *1\. Enterprises with losses may carry forward their losses to the following year; this loss is deducted from taxable income. The period for carrying forward losses shall not exceed 05 consecutive years, starting from the year following the year in which the loss arose.*
> 
> *2\. Enterprises that incur losses from the transfer of mineral exploration and exploitation projects; transfer of rights to participate in mineral exploration, exploitation and processing projects; transfer of rights to explore, exploit and process minerals may transfer losses to the following year into the taxable income of such activities. The time for transferring losses is prescribed in Clause 1 of this Article.*
> 
> *3\. The Government shall detail this Article.*

Accordingly, loss carryover from October 2025 is regulated as follows:

**(i)** Enterprises with losses may carry forward their losses to the following year; this loss is deducted from taxable income. The period for carrying forward losses shall not exceed 5 consecutive years, starting from the year following the year in which the loss arose.

**(ii)** Enterprises with losses from the transfer of mineral exploration and exploitation projects; transfer of mineral exploration, exploitation and processing rights; transfer of rights to participate in mineral exploration, exploitation and processing projects may transfer losses to the following year into the taxable income of such activities. The time for transferring losses is as prescribed in item (i) above.

## 2\. Method of calculating corporate income tax

Pursuant to Article 11 [of the Law on Corporate Income Tax 2025,](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Luat-Thue-thu-nhap-doanh-nghiep-2025-so-67-2025-QH15-580594.aspx?anchor=dieu_11) the tax calculation method is prescribed as follows:

**(i)** The amount of corporate income tax payable in the tax period is calculated by multiplying taxable income by the tax rate, except for the case specified in Clause 2, Article 11 [of the Law on Corporate Income Tax 2025](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Luat-Thue-thu-nhap-doanh-nghiep-2025-so-67-2025-QH15-580594.aspx?anchor=dieu_11).

**(ii)** The Government prescribes the amount of corporate income tax payable calculated as a percentage of revenue in the following cases:

\- Enterprises specified in Point c and Point d, Clause 2, Article 2 [of the Law on Corporate Income Tax 2025](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Luat-Thue-thu-nhap-doanh-nghiep-2025-so-67-2025-QH15-580594.aspx?anchor=dieu_2); subjects performing the obligation to declare and pay taxes, time and method of determining revenue to calculate taxable income arising in Vietnam.

\- Enterprises with total annual revenue not exceeding VND 3 billion as prescribed in Clause 2, Article 10 [of the Law on Corporate Income Tax 2025](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Luat-Thue-thu-nhap-doanh-nghiep-2025-so-67-2025-QH15-580594.aspx?anchor=dieu_10) in cases where revenue can be determined but costs and income of production and business activities cannot be determined.

\- Cooperatives, cooperative unions, public service units and other organizations specified in Points c, d and dd, Clause 1, Article 2 [of the Law on Corporate Income Tax 2025](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Luat-Thue-thu-nhap-doanh-nghiep-2025-so-67-2025-QH15-580594.aspx?anchor=dieu_2) that have production and business activities of goods and services with income subject to corporate income tax (except for tax-exempt income specified in Article 4 [of the Law on Corporate Income Tax 2025](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Luat-Thue-thu-nhap-doanh-nghiep-2025-so-67-2025-QH15-580594.aspx?anchor=dieu_4) ) and these units can account for revenue but cannot determine the costs and income of production and business activities.

## 3\. Corporate income tax rate incentives from October 2025

Pursuant to Article 13 [of the Law on Corporate Income Tax 2025](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Luat-Thue-thu-nhap-doanh-nghiep-2025-so-67-2025-QH15-580594.aspx?anchor=dieu_13), from October 2025, there will be changes in incentives on corporate income tax rates as follows:

(i) 10% tax incentive for 15 years.

(ii) 10% tax incentive for the entire period of operation.

(iii) 15% tax incentive for the entire period of operation.

(iv) 17% tax incentive for 10 years.

(v) 17% tax incentive for the entire period of operation.

(vi) Extend the period of application of preferential tax rates.
