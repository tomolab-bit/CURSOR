## Decree 236/2025/ND-CP has been issued to guide Resolution 107 on applying global minimum tax.

On August 29, 2025, the Government issued Decree 236/2025/ND-CP detailing a number of articles of [Resolution 107/2023/QH15](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Nghi-quyet-107-2023-QH15-ap-dung-thue-thu-nhap-bo-sung-chong-xoi-mon-co-so-thue-toan-cau-578567.aspx) on the application of additional corporate income tax under the provisions on preventing global tax base erosion.

| [![](https://news.thuvienphapluat.vn/tintuc/content/images/tv.png)](https://drive.google.com/file/d/15f-aWlwlYNq7rnauRuNYxiBOwVN3mReO/view "Download") | **Decree 236/2025/ND-CP** |
| --- | --- |

Accordingly, this Decree details a number of articles of [Resolution 107/2023/QH15](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Nghi-quyet-107-2023-QH15-ap-dung-thue-thu-nhap-bo-sung-chong-xoi-mon-co-so-thue-toan-cau-578567.aspx) on the application of additional corporate income tax under the provisions on preventing global tax base erosion (hereinafter referred to as the Regulation on global minimum tax).

The taxpayer is a constituent unit of a multinational corporation with annual revenue in the consolidated financial statements of the ultimate parent company of at least 02 years in the 04 consecutive years preceding the fiscal year determining the tax liability equivalent to 750 million euros (EUR) or more, excluding the exclusions specified in Clause 1, Article 2 of Resolution [107/2023/QH15](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Nghi-quyet-107-2023-QH15-ap-dung-thue-thu-nhap-bo-sung-chong-xoi-mon-co-so-thue-toan-cau-578567.aspx) and Clause 3 of this Article. The constituent unit specified in this Clause shall comply with the provisions in Clause 7, Article 3 [of Resolution 107/2023/QH15](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Nghi-quyet-107-2023-QH15-ap-dung-thue-thu-nhap-bo-sung-chong-xoi-mon-co-so-thue-toan-cau-578567.aspx).

In case a newly established multinational corporation has been in operation for less than 04 years prior to the fiscal year determining tax liability, if there are at least 02 years with annual revenue in the consolidated financial statements of the ultimate parent company equivalent to 750 million EUR or more, then the constituent unit of that multinational corporation is the taxpayer.

Excluded cases, which are not taxpayers include:

\- Cases specified from point a to point c, clause 1, Article 2 [of Resolution 107/2023/QH15](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Nghi-quyet-107-2023-QH15-ap-dung-thue-thu-nhap-bo-sung-chong-xoi-mon-co-so-thue-toan-cau-578567.aspx). The determination of cases excluded at this point is based on the provisions at points 5 to 9, Appendix 1; point 10.1, point 10.4, Section III, Appendix II; clause 4, Article 3 of [Resolution 107/2023/QH15](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Nghi-quyet-107-2023-QH15-ap-dung-thue-thu-nhap-bo-sung-chong-xoi-mon-co-so-thue-toan-cau-578567.aspx) and point 2, Appendix 1.

\- The cases specified in Point g, Clause 1, Article 2 [of Resolution 107/2023/QH15](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Nghi-quyet-107-2023-QH15-ap-dung-thue-thu-nhap-bo-sung-chong-xoi-mon-co-so-thue-toan-cau-578567.aspx) include:

\+ An entity in which at least 95% of its value is owned directly or through one or more entities excluded in Point a of this Clause (except for pension service organizations) and its operations fall into one of the following two cases or a combination of both:

++ That entity operates solely or principally for the purpose of holding assets or investing capital for the benefit of excluded entities;

++ That entity only performs activities that are ancillary to activities performed by excluded entities or by third parties owned by the excluded entity (at least 95% of the entity's value);

\+ An entity in which at least 85% of the value of the entity is owned directly or through one or more excluded entities specified in Point a of this Clause (except for pension service organizations) provided that most of the entity's income is excluded dividends or is profit or loss on equity that is excluded when calculating income or loss as prescribed in Point 4.1.2 and Point 4.1.3, Section II, Appendix II.

\- The determination of an excluded entity under the provisions of Point b of this Clause is based on the entire activities of this entity, including the activities of its permanent establishment. In case an entity is determined to be an excluded entity, the entire activities of these entities, including the activities carried out by its permanent establishment, shall be excluded under the Global Minimum Tax Regulation.

\- A unit that is a member of a corporation and owned by an investment fund or owned by a real estate investment organization that meets the conditions prescribed in Point b of this Clause shall be determined as an excluded unit even though the investment fund or real estate investment organization is not the ultimate parent company of that corporation.

\- In case a unit specified in point 6.1.2 of this clause whose entire value is owned directly or indirectly by a non-profit organization, the operation of this unit is determined as an auxiliary operation if the total revenue of the members of the group (excluding revenue from the non-profit organization or revenue from an excluded unit as prescribed in points b.1.1 and b.2 of this clause or another unit considered an excluded unit as prescribed in this point) is lower than 25% of the revenue of the multinational group and lower than 750 million EUR (in case the fiscal year is different from 12 months, the provisions in point a, clause 2 of this Article shall apply).

\- Revenue of excluded units will be included in group revenue when determining the consolidated revenue threshold.

\- The constituent unit responsible for declaration may elect to waive the right to apply Clause 3 of this Article to a unit that is an excluded unit. The election under this Clause shall apply according to the provisions on the 5-year election.

See details in Decree 236/2025/ND-CP effective from October 15, 2025.
