## 1\. Organizational structure and operation of capital safety ratio management with commercial banks from September 15, 2025

The Governor of the State Bank of Vietnam issued [Circular 14/2025/TT-NHNN](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-14-2025-TT-NHNN-ty-le-an-toan-von-doi-voi-ngan-hang-thuong-mai-632909.aspx) regulating capital safety ratios for commercial banks and foreign bank branches.

Accordingly, the organizational structure and operation of capital safety ratio management for commercial banks and foreign bank branches are as follows:

\- Capital adequacy ratio management activities:

\+ The Board of Directors, Board of Members (for commercial banks) or General Director (Director) (for foreign bank branches) shall:

(i) Issue internal regulations on compliance with the bank's minimum capital adequacy ratio in accordance with the needs, characteristics, level of risk in operations, business cycles, ability to adapt to risks and business strategies of the bank; in accordance with this Circular and relevant legal provisions;

(ii) Supervision of the General Director (Director) (for commercial banks) in implementing capital adequacy ratio management;

\+ General Director (Director) performs:

(i) Develop and submit to the Board of Directors and Board of Members (for commercial banks) for promulgation internal regulations on compliance with minimum capital adequacy ratio;

(ii) Managing capital adequacy ratios to ensure compliance with internal regulations;

(iii) Supervision of individuals and departments performing the function of capital safety ratio management;

\+ Have an individual or department performing the function of managing the capital safety ratio, submitting it to the General Director (Director) to report to the Board of Directors, Board of Members (for commercial banks) or submitting it to the General Director (Director) (for foreign bank branches) according to internal regulations on compliance with the bank's minimum capital safety ratio in accordance with this Circular and relevant legal provisions.

\- The Board of Supervisors of commercial banks must supervise the compliance with regulations on capital safety ratio in [Circular 14/2025/TT-NHNN](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-14-2025-TT-NHNN-ty-le-an-toan-von-doi-voi-ngan-hang-thuong-mai-632909.aspx).

\- The bank's internal audit conducts independent and objective reviews and assessments of the appropriateness and compliance with internal regulations on capital adequacy ratio management of the bank.

See more in [Circular 14/2025/TT-NHNN](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-14-2025-TT-NHNN-ty-le-an-toan-von-doi-voi-ngan-hang-thuong-mai-632909.aspx) effective from September 15, 2025, except for the case in the period from the effective date [of Circular 14/2025/TT-NHNN to December 31, 2029, in case of not registering to implement the standard method as prescribed in Article 7](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-14-2025-TT-NHNN-ty-le-an-toan-von-doi-voi-ngan-hang-thuong-mai-632909.aspx) [of Circular 14/2025/TT-NHNN](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-14-2025-TT-NHNN-ty-le-an-toan-von-doi-voi-ngan-hang-thuong-mai-632909.aspx) or not yet approved by the State Bank to apply the internal rating method as prescribed in Article 33 [of Circular 14/2025/TT-NHNN](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-14-2025-TT-NHNN-ty-le-an-toan-von-doi-voi-ngan-hang-thuong-mai-632909.aspx), the bank must comply with the provisions in the Circulars specified in Points a, b and c, Clause 3, Article 82 [of Circular 14/2025/TT-NHNN](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-14-2025-TT-NHNN-ty-le-an-toan-von-doi-voi-ngan-hang-thuong-mai-632909.aspx).

## 2\. Approval authority for microfinance institution network from September 15, 2025

The Governor of the State Bank of Vietnam issued [Circular 19/2025/TT-NHNN](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-19-2025-TT-NHNN-mang-luoi-hoat-dong-cua-to-chuc-tai-chinh-vi-mo-668317.aspx) dated July 31, 2025 regulating the operating network of microfinance institutions, effective from September 15, 2025, replacing [Circular 19/2019/TT-NHNN](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-19-2019-TT-NHNN-mang-luoi-hoat-dong-cua-to-chuc-tai-chinh-vi-mo-428431.aspx).

Accordingly, the approval authority for the network of microfinance institutions is as follows:

\- Authority of the Governor of the State Bank of Vietnam:

\+ Approval of establishment of branches of microfinance institutions;

\+ Mandatory termination of operations and dissolution of branches of microfinance institutions;

\+ Approve the contents of the microfinance institution's operational network to implement the approved restructuring plan for the specially controlled microfinance institution based on the procedures prescribed in this Circular.

\- Authority of the Director of the Department of Credit Institution Management and Supervision:

\+ Approve the establishment of transaction offices, representative offices, and public service units of microfinance institutions;

\+ Mandatory termination of operations, dissolution of transaction offices, representative offices, and public service units of microfinance institutions.

\- Authority of the Director of the State Bank Regional Branch:

\+ Approve changes in location of branch headquarters and transaction offices of microfinance institutions in the area, including cases of location changes before opening operations;

\+ Approve the voluntary termination of operations and dissolution of branches and transaction offices of microfinance institutions.

## 3\. Information groups and reports provided to the Monitoring Information System to serve the monitoring of people's credit fund activities from September 15, 2025

The Governor of the State Bank of Vietnam issued Circular 18/2025/TT-NHNN dated July 31, 2025 regulating the collection, exploitation, sharing of information and reports of the Information System serving the supervision of the activities of people's credit funds and microfinance institutions.

Specifically, the Governor of the State Bank shall issue a List of information and reports provided to the Monitoring Information System in each period, including the following groups of information and reports:

\- Information and reports provided by people's credit funds and microfinance organizations include:

\+ Identification information about borrowers and depositors;

\+ Information about related persons of the borrower;

\+ Information on lending activities;

\+ Personnel information;

\+ Member information;

\+ Information on loan security measures;

\+ Information on on-balance sheet bad debt handling activities;

\+ Off-balance sheet information;

\+ Financial report;

\+ Other information.

\- Information and reports provided by the Department of Credit Institution System Safety, the State Bank Inspectorate, the Department of Credit Institution Management and Supervision, the State Bank Regional Branch where the people's credit fund has its head office (hereinafter referred to as the State Bank Regional Branch).

\- Information from information systems managed by units under the State Bank:

\+ State Bank statistical reporting system;

\+ Information system supporting remote monitoring activities;

\+ Credit information system;

\+ Other information systems.

## 4\. Members of the Board of Supervisors for the destruction of money that does not meet circulation standards from September 15, 2025

The Governor of the State Bank of Vietnam issued [Circular 22/2025/TT-NHNN](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-22-2025-TT-NHNN-sua-doi-Thong-tu-19-2023-TT-NHNN-giam-sat-tieu-huy-tien-670247.aspx) dated August 1, 2025 amending [Circular 19/2023/TT-NHNN](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-19-2023-TT-NHNN-giam-sat-tieu-huy-tien-593076.aspx) regulating the supervision of money destruction by the State Bank of Vietnam, effective from September 15, 2025.

Accordingly, the members of the Council for supervising the destruction of money that does not meet circulation standards include:

\- Chairman of the Supervisory Board: Chief Inspector of the State Bank;

\- 01 (one) Vice Chairman of the Supervisory Council is the Deputy Chief Inspector of the State Bank, directly in charge of the Northern Destruction Supervision Cluster;

\- 01 (one) Vice Chairman of the Supervisory Board is the leader of the State Bank Branch, Region 2, directly in charge of the Southern Destruction Supervision Cluster;

\- 01 (one) member is the leader of the Office participating in the Northern Destruction Monitoring Cluster;

\- 01 (one) member is a department-level leader of the Office, participating in the Southern Destruction Monitoring Cluster;

\- 01 (one) member is a department-level leader of the State Bank Inspectorate, concurrently the secretary of the Supervisory Council and secretary of the Northern Destruction Supervision Cluster;

\- 01 (one) member is a department-level leader of the State Bank, Region 2 branch, concurrently the secretary of the Southern Destruction Supervision Cluster.

## 5\. Payment methods in Vietnam - China border trade activities

The Governor of the State Bank of Vietnam issued [Circular 17/2025/TT-NHNN](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-17-2025-TT-NHNN-sua-doi-Thong-tu-19-2018-TT-NHNN-667568.aspx) dated July 30, 2025 amending [Circular 19/2018/TT-NHNN](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-19-2018-TT-NHNN-quan-ly-ngoai-hoi-hoat-dong-thuong-mai-bien-gioi-Viet-Nam-Trung-Quoc-392543.aspx) guiding foreign exchange management for Vietnam - China border trade activities, effective from September 15, 2025.

Accordingly, [Circular 17/2025/TT-NHNN](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-17-2025-TT-NHNN-sua-doi-Thong-tu-19-2018-TT-NHNN-667568.aspx) amends and supplements Article 4 [of Circular 19/2018/TT-NHNN](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Tien-te-Ngan-hang/Thong-tu-19-2018-TT-NHNN-quan-ly-ngoai-hoi-hoat-dong-thuong-mai-bien-gioi-Viet-Nam-Trung-Quoc-392543.aspx) on payment methods in Vietnam - China border trade activities as follows:

\- Payment via bank, including:

\+ Payment in freely convertible foreign currency through authorized banks according to current regulations on foreign exchange management;

\+ Payment in CNY via border bank branches;

\+ Payment in VND through border bank branches.

\- Clearing payment (payment of the difference in the transaction of clearing exported goods and services with imported goods and services in Vietnam - China border trade activities) through licensed banks.
