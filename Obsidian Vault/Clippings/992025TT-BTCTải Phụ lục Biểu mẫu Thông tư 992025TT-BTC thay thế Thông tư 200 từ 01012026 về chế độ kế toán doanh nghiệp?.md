## Regulations on accounting voucher forms system from January 1, 2026

Article 9 of Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC stipulates the system of accounting voucher forms from January 1, 2026 as follows:

\- Enterprises can refer to and apply the accounting voucher system form in Appendix 1 issued with Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC.

\- In order to suit the characteristics of production and business activities and management requirements, enterprises are allowed to design additional or modify and supplement accounting voucher forms compared to the guidance forms in Appendix I issued with Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC.

When designing or amending or supplementing an enterprise's accounting voucher form, it must comply with the provisions of Article 16 of the Law on Accounting and must fully, promptly, honestly, transparently, easily check, control and compare the assets and capital of the enterprise. When designing or amending or supplementing an accounting voucher form, the enterprise is responsible for issuing the Accounting Regulations (or equivalent documents) on the amended or supplemented contents to serve as a basis for implementation.

The regulations must clearly state the necessity of such amendments and supplements and the responsibility of the enterprise before the law for the amended and supplemented contents. In case the enterprise does not design or amend or supplement the accounting voucher form, it shall apply the accounting voucher system guided in Appendix 1 issued with Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC.

\- If an enterprise generates documents that are subject to regulation by other laws, the accounting documents must comply with the provisions of that law.

## Regulations on accounting system from January 1, 2026

Pursuant to Article 11 of Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC regulating the accounting account system from January 1, 2026 as follows:

\- Enterprises apply the accounting system in Appendix II issued with Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC to serve the accounting recording of economic transactions arising at the enterprise.

\- In order to suit the characteristics of production and business activities and management requirements of the unit, the enterprise may amend and supplement the name, number, structure and reflection content of the accounting accounts as guided in Appendix II issued with Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC.

The amendments and supplements must ensure the classification and systematization of arising transactions according to economic content, no duplication of subjects, compliance with accounting principles as prescribed and must not change or affect the indicators and information presented in the Financial Report. When amending and supplementing the names, numbers, structures and contents of accounting accounts, enterprises are responsible for issuing accounting regulations (or equivalent documents) on the amended and supplemented contents to serve as a basis for implementation.

The regulations must clearly state the necessity of such amendments and supplements and the responsibility of the enterprise before the law for the amended and supplemented contents. In case the enterprise does not amend or supplement the name, number, structure and reflection content of the accounting accounts, the accounting account system as guided in Appendix II issued with Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC shall apply.

\- Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC only provides guidance on the content and accounting methods of some major economic transactions. In case an enterprise has economic transactions that have not been guided by accounting in this Circular, the enterprise shall base on the content and nature of the arising economic transactions, the provisions of the Accounting Law, documents guiding the Accounting Law, Vietnamese Accounting Standards and the guiding principles in this Circular to implement.
