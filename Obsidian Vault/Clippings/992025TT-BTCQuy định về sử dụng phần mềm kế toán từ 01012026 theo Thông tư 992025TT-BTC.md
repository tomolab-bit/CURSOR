Enterprises using accounting software should note: The Ministry of Finance has issued regulations on the use of accounting software from January 1, 2026 according to [Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC replacing Circular 200/2014/TT-BTC](https://amis.misa.vn/251383/thong-tu-99-2025-tt-btc-thay-the-thong-tu-200-2014-tt-btc/?lang=en). Join **MISA AMIS** to learn more about this regulation in the section below.

## 1\. Regulations on the use of accounting software from January 1, 2026

Pursuant to Article 28, Enterprises may use accounting software to perform accounting work as prescribed in Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC. The accounting software that enterprises choose to use must ensure minimum professional and technical requirements in accounting.

### 1.1. Minimum requirements for accounting software from January 1, 2026

The accounting software used must ensure the following requirements:

1. The accounting processes and operations established on the software must ensure compliance with the provisions of accounting law, tax law and other relevant laws, and **must not change the nature, principles, accounting methods and information and data** presented in accounting books and financial statements as prescribed.
2. The processing of accounting procedures, information, and related data must ensure accuracy, consistency, and no duplication. When making corrections, traces of the recorded accounting contents must be kept in chronological order.
3. Information and data on accounting software must ensure confidentiality, security and must comply with legal regulations on information security and safety. The information system is set up to be able to warn or prevent intentional interference that changes information and data recorded in accounting books.
4. Provide complete and timely information and output data as required by competent authorities and data and information users.
5. Able to connect or ready to connect with relevant software when performing accounting work (electronic invoice software, digital signature, etc.).
6. Able to upgrade, modify and supplement in accordance with changes in accounting, tax and other relevant laws.

### 1.2. Related responsibilities of accounting software users

Business managers, chief accountants/accounting managers and relevant persons are ***responsible for the accuracy and truthfulness*** of accounting information and data provided from accounting software.

## 2\. Regulations on accounting organization and accounting work from January 1, 2026

Regulations on the organization of the accounting apparatus and accounting work are detailed in Article 7 of Circular No. 99/2025/TT-BTC (effective from January 1, 2026) and general regulations related to internal management in Article 3 of this Circular.

**1\. Responsibilities and Decision-Making Rights of the Enterprise:**

Enterprises are responsible for organizing their accounting apparatus and deciding on accounting work for their affiliated units. This organization must be consistent with the characteristics of their production and business activities, management requirements of their units, and must not be contrary to the provisions of law.

**2\. Organization of accounting apparatus and accounting work at units under the enterprise**

| Enterprises are entitled to delegate to their affiliated units the right to record the capital that the enterprise provides to its affiliated units as liabilities or equity, to record or not to record revenue and cost of goods when circulating products, goods and services between internal stages without depending on the form of accounting documents (invoices or internal circulation documents) in accordance with the model and requirements for managing the enterprise's production and business activities; |
| --- |
| Enterprises **have the right to delegate to their affiliated units to prepare Financial Reports or not to prepare Financial Reports**. However, the Financial Reports of enterprises when submitted to competent authorities or made public according to regulations must include financial information of both the head office and the affiliated units of the enterprise regardless of whether the enterprise has delegated to its affiliated units to prepare Financial Reports or not. |
