**Example**[: Thien Ung Accounting](http://ketoanthienung.net/ "Eagle Accounting") Company purchased a fixed asset (100% new) with the invoice price of 119 million VND, purchase discount of 5 million VND, transportation cost of 3 million VND, installation and testing cost of 3 million VND.a. Knowing that the fixed asset has a technical life of 12 years, the depreciation period of the enterprise's fixed asset  
  
is expected to be 10 years *(in accordance with the provisions in Appendix 1 issued with Circular No. 45/2013/TT-BTC)*, the asset was put into use on January 1, 2013.\- Original price of fixed asset = 119 million - 5 million + 3 million + 3 million = 120 million VND \- Average annual depreciation rate = 120 million: 10 years = 12 million VND/year.\- Average monthly depreciation rate = 12 million VND: 12 months = 1 million VND/month \- Every year, the enterprise deducts 12 million VND in depreciation costs for that fixed asset into business expenses. **b.** **After 5 years of use** **,** the enterprise upgrades the fixed asset with a total cost of 30 million VND, the re-evaluated usage time is 6 years ( increased by 1 year compared to the initially registered usage time ), the completion date for putting into use is January 1, 2018.\- Original price of fixed assets = 120 million VND + 30 million VND = 150 million VND \- Accumulated depreciation = 12 million VND (x) 5 years = 60 million VND \- Remaining value on accounting books = 150 million VND - 60 million VND = 90 million VND \- Average annual depreciation rate = 90 million VND: 6 years = 15 million VND/year \- Average monthly depreciation rate = 15,000,000 VND: 12 months = 1,250,000 VND/month From 2018 onwards,the enterprise will deduct 1,250,000 VND into business expenses each month for newly upgraded fixed assets.
