## What is the structure and content of Account 611 - Purchase?

The structure and content of account 611 - Purchases are specified in Clause 2, Article 83 [of Circular 200/2014/TT-BTC](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Thong-tu-200-2014-TT-BTC-huong-dan-Che-do-ke-toan-Doanh-nghiep-263599.aspx?anchor=dieu_83) as follows:

**Debit:**

\- Carry over the original cost of goods, raw materials, materials, tools, and equipment in inventory at the beginning of the period (according to inventory results);

\- Original price of goods, raw materials, materials, tools, and equipment purchased during the period;

**Creditor:**

\- Carry over the original price of goods, raw materials, materials, tools, and equipment in inventory at the end of the period (according to inventory results);

\- Original price of goods, raw materials, materials, tools, and instruments used during the period, or original price of goods sold (not yet determined as sold during the period);

\- Original price of raw materials, materials, tools, equipment, and purchased goods returned to the seller or discounted.

**Account 611 has no ending balance.**

***Account 611 - Purchase, has 2 sub-accounts:***

\- Account 6111 - Purchase of raw materials and supplies: This account is used to reflect the value of raw materials, supplies, tools, and equipment purchased and used during the accounting period and to transfer the value of raw materials, supplies, tools, and equipment in inventory at the beginning and end of the accounting period;

\- Account 6112 - Purchase of goods: This account is used to reflect the value of goods purchased and sold during the accounting period and transfer the value of goods in inventory at the beginning and end of the accounting period.

## The value of purchased goods reflected in Account 611 - Purchase must follow which principle?

Pursuant to the provisions of Point a, Clause 1, Article 83 [of Circular 200/2014/TT-BTC](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Thong-tu-200-2014-TT-BTC-huong-dan-Che-do-ke-toan-Doanh-nghiep-263599.aspx?anchor=dieu_83) as follows:

> ***Account 611 - Purchases***

> *1\. Accounting principles*

> *a) This account is used to reflect the value of raw materials, materials, tools, equipment, and goods purchased, stored, or put into use during the period. Account 611 "Purchases" only applies to businesses that account for inventories using the periodic inventory method.*

> ***b) The value of purchased raw materials, materials, tools, equipment, and goods reflected in account 611 "Purchases" must be carried out according to the original cost principle.***

> *c) In case of accounting for inventories using the periodic inventory method, the enterprise must organize an inventory count at the end of the accounting period to determine the quantity and value of each raw material, material, goods, product, tool, and instrument in inventory at the end of the accounting period to determine the value of inventories used and sold during the period.*

> *...*

Accordingly, the value of purchased goods reflected in Account 611 - Purchases must be made according to the original cost principle.

## What is the accounting method for Account 611 for businesses trading in goods?

Pursuant to the provisions of Clause 3, Article 83 [of Circular 200/2014/TT-BTC](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Thong-tu-200-2014-TT-BTC-huong-dan-Che-do-ke-toan-Doanh-nghiep-263599.aspx?anchor=dieu_83), the accounting method for some main economic transactions in Account 611 - Purchase of goods for enterprises trading in goods is as follows:

**(1)** At the beginning of the accounting period, transfer the value of beginning inventory, record:

Debit account 611 - Purchases (6112)

Credit account 156 - Goods.

**(2)** During the accounting period, when purchasing goods, if input VAT is deductible, based on invoices and purchase documents:

\- Actual value of purchased goods, recorded:

Debit account 611 - Purchases (6112)

Debit account 133 - Deductible VAT (1331) (if any)

Have accounts 111, 112, 141; or

Credit account 331 - Payable to sellers (total payment price).

\- Actual purchase costs incurred, record:

Debit account 611 - Purchases (6112)

Debit account 133 - Deductible VAT (1331) (if any)

There are accounts 111, 112, 141, 331,...

\- When paying early, if the business receives a payment discount on the purchased shipment, record:

Debit account 331 - Payable to sellers (deducted from payables to sellers)

There are accounts 111, 112,...

Credit account 515 - Financial activity revenue.

\- Value of goods returned to the seller, record:

Debit accounts 111, 112 (if collected immediately in cash)

Debit account 331 - Payable to sellers (deducted from payables to sellers)

Credit account 611 - Purchases (6112) (value of goods returned to seller)

Credit account 133 - Deductible VAT (1331) (if any).

\- Discount on purchased goods approved by the seller due to goods not meeting the quality and specifications as per the contract, recorded:

Debit accounts 111, 112 (if collected immediately in cash)

Debit account 331 - Payable to sellers (deducted from payables to sellers)

Credit account 611 - Purchases (6112)

Credit account 133 - Deductible VAT (1331) (if any).

**(3)** At the end of the accounting period, based on the results of the actual inventory, determine the value of inventory, the value of goods sent for sale but not yet determined as sold, and the value of goods determined as sold:

\- Transfer the value of inventory and goods sent for sale at the end of the period, record:

Debit account 156 - Goods

Debit account 157 - Goods sent for sale

Credit account 611 - Purchases.

\- Transfer cost of goods sold, record:

Debit account 632 - Cost of goods sold

Credit account 611 - Purchases (6112).
