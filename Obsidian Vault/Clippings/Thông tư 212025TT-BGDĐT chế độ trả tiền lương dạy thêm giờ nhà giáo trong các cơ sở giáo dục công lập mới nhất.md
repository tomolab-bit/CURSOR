Education and Training issued a Circular regulating the regime of overtime pay for teachers in public educational institutions.</font></font></i></p><p><a><b><font><font>Article 1. Scope of regulation</font></font></b></a></p><p><font><font>1. This Circular stipulates the regime of overtime pay </font></font><font><font>for teachers in public educational institutions in the national education system.</font></font></p><p><font><font>2. This Circular does not apply to teachers in educational institutions under the Ministry of National Defense and the Ministry of Public Security.</font></font></p><p><a><b><font><font>Article 2. Subjects of application</font></font></b></a></p><p><font><font>1. Teachers and teachers holding the position of educational institution management staff (hereinafter referred to as teachers) on the salary list approved by competent authorities, currently teaching in public educational institutions in the national education system.</font></font></p><p><font><font>2. Teachers on the payroll approved by competent authorities, currently instructing practice at workshops, stations, camps, laboratories in public educational institutions or instructing practice on training ships.</font></font></p><p><a><b><font><font>Article 3. General regulations on calculating overtime pay</font></font></b></a></p><p><font><font>1. The standard teaching hours/school year for preschool teachers; the standard teaching period/school year for general, regular, and pre-university teachers; the standard teaching hours/school year for vocational education teachers; the standard teaching hours/school year for lecturers at higher education institutions, training and fostering institutions of ministries, ministerial-level agencies, government agencies, political organizations, socio-political organizations, and political schools of provinces and centrally-run cities are collectively referred to as the standard teaching period/school year.</font></font></p><p><font><font>2. The monthly salary used as the basis for calculating overtime pay for teachers includes: salary calculated according to salary coefficient (or according to job position), leadership position allowance (if any), salary allowances and reserve difference coefficient (if any).</font></font></p><p><font><font>3. Total number of extra teaching hours per school year for each teacher</font></font></p><p><font><font>a) The total number of extra teaching hours in a school year of each teacher calculated to pay overtime wages shall not exceed 200 hours, except for the case specified in Point b of this Clause;</font></font></p><p><font><font>b) In case the total number of extra teaching periods in a school year of a teacher exceeds 200 periods due to the subject not being able to arrange enough teachers to teach, the head of the educational institution must notify the competent authority in writing.</font></font></p><p><font><font>4. Total number of extra teaching hours </font></font><font><font>in a school year of all teachers</font></font></p><p><font><font>a) The total number of extra teaching periods </font></font><font><font>in a school year of all teachers must not be higher than the </font></font><font><font>maximum total number of extra teaching periods in a school year of the educational institution specified in </font></font><a><font><font>Clause 1, Article 4 of this Circular,</font></font></a><font><font> except for the case specified in Point b of this Clause;</font></font></p><p><font><font>b) In case the total number of extra teaching periods in a school year of all teachers in an educational institution is higher than the </font></font><font><font>maximum total number of extra teaching periods in a school year of the educational institution due to a subject for which there are not enough teachers to teach, the head of the educational institution must notify in writing the educational management agency according to its management authority.</font></font></p><p><font><font>5. Tasks that have received remuneration or allowances cannot be converted into teaching hours or have their teaching hours reduced to calculate overtime pay, unless otherwise provided by law.</font></font></p><p><font><font>6. The school year specified in this Circular is calculated from July 1 of the previous year to June 30 of the following year (including summer vacation).</font></font></p><p><font><font>In case of sudden, urgent cases to prevent and combat natural disasters, epidemics or urgent cases requiring adjustment of the school year schedule, the start time and end time of the school year shall be determined according to the adjustment document for that school year of the competent authority.</font></font></p><p><font><font>7. The time for paying overtime pay to teachers is after the end of the school year. In case a teacher retires, quits, is transferred, the payment of overtime pay is made at the time of the decision on retirement, quits, is transferred, or transferred by the competent authority.</font></font></p><p><font><font>8. Teachers working in public educational institutions with many levels of education and training are subject to the prescribed teaching hours/school year norms for the level of education and training corresponding to the professional title of the appointed teacher.</font></font></p><p><font><font>9. The time when a teacher does not directly participate in teaching but is counted as completing the required number of teaching periods according to the prescribed norms and is included in the total number of teaching periods in the school year of the teacher, including:</font></font></p><p><font><font>a) Time for teachers to perform other tasks assigned or mobilized by educational institutions or competent authorities (except for tasks for which remuneration has been received);</font></font></p><p><font><font>b) Time when teachers are sent for training and development by competent authorities without directly participating in teaching;</font></font></p><p><font><font>c) Time is calculated to complete the required number of teaching periods according to the provisions of law.</font></font></p><p><font><font>10. Overtime pay for teachers on secondment or inter-school teaching is determined as follows:</font></font></p><p><font><font>a) The educational institution where the teacher is on secondment shall pay the overtime salary for the seconded teacher;</font></font></p><p><font><font>b) The educational institution where the teacher goes to teach inter-school shall pay the teacher extra teaching hours. In case the teacher is assigned to teach inter-school at three or more educational institutions at the same time (including the educational institution where the teacher works), the teacher's extra teaching hours shall be paid by the educational institutions where the teacher goes to teach inter-school according to the ratio of the teacher's actual teaching hours at these educational institutions.</font></font></p><p><a><b><font><font>Article 4. Total number of extra teaching periods in a school year</font></font></b></a></p><p><font><font>1. The maximum </font><font>total number of extra teaching hours in a school year for which an educational institution is paid overtime wages </font></font><font><font>is determined as follows:</font></font></p><p><font><font>Total maximum number of extra teaching periods </font></font><font><font>in a school year = [Total number of teaching periods of subjects, modules, modules, educational activities of all classes according to the education program/school year or training program/school year + Total number of teaching periods calculated to perform other tasks/school year (if any) + Total number of teaching periods calculated to be fully taught/school year (if any)] - (Total teaching period norms of all teachers/school year).</font></font></p><p><font><font>In there:</font></font></p><p><font><font>a) The total number of teaching periods calculated to perform other tasks/school year includes: the total number of converted teaching periods/school year according to regulations; the total number of additional teaching periods/school year according to regulations; the total number of reduced teaching periods/school year according to regulations;</font></font></p><p><font><font>b) Total teaching hour norms of all teachers/school year include: total teaching hour norms/school year of all teachers working for a full school year (excluding teachers sent to teach inter-school); total actual teaching hours of teachers sent to teach inter-school (if any)/school year at the educational institution where the teacher works; total teaching hour norms/actual working time for cases of working for less than a full school year (including teachers sent on assignment and teachers sent on secondment).</font></font></p><p><font><font>The standard teaching period/actual working time is calculated according to the provisions at </font></font><a><font><font>Point a, Point b, Point c, Clause 2, Article 6 of this Circular</font></font></a><font><font>.</font></font></p><p><font><font>2. The total number of extra teaching hours </font></font><font><font>in a school year for teachers is determined as follows:</font></font></p><p><font><font>Number of extra teaching hours </font></font><font><font>of teachers/school year = (Total number of actual teaching hours/school year) - (Standard teaching hours/school year).</font></font></p><p><font><font>In there:</font></font></p><p><font><font>a) The total number of actual teaching periods/school year includes: actual number of teaching periods/school year; converted teaching periods/school year according to regulations (if any); additional teaching periods/school year according to regulations (if any); reduced teaching periods/school year according to regulations (if any); and teaching periods counted towards the required number of teaching periods/school year according to regulations (if any).</font></font></p><p><font><font>For teachers assigned to teach on a secondment or inter-school basis, the actual number of teaching periods/school year is the total actual number of teaching periods/school year at all educational institutions where the teacher participates in teaching;</font></font></p><p><font><font>b) Standard teaching hours/school year of preschool teachers = (Standard number of teaching hours/day) x (Number of working days/week) x (Number of weeks teaching children/school year);</font></font></p><p><font><font>c) The teaching hours/school year norms for teachers at other educational institutions are implemented according to current regulations on teachers' working regime;</font></font></p><p><font><font>d) In case there are no regulations on the teaching hours/school year norms for teachers, the head of the educational institution shall stipulate them in writing after agreeing with the collective of teachers and management staff of the educational institution.</font></font></p><p><font><font>3. Based on the total </font></font><font><font>maximum number of extra teaching hours in a school year for which the educational institution is paid overtime wages </font></font><font><font>and the number of extra teaching hours </font></font><font><font>of each teacher, the head of the educational institution decides the number of extra teaching hours </font></font><font><font>of each teacher to be paid, ensuring the principles specified in </font></font><a><font><font>Clause 3, Article 3 of this Circular</font></font></a><font><font>.</font></font></p><p><a><b><font><font>Article 5.
