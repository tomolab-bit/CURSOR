## 2\. Hồ sơ khai thuế TNCN đối với thu nhập từ chuyển nhượng vốn góp

Căn cứ Mục 9.4 của Phụ lục I ban hành kèm theo [Nghị định 126/2020/NĐ-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Nghi-dinh-126-2020-ND-CP-huong-dan-Luat-Quan-ly-thue-455733.aspx), hồ sơ khai thuế TNCN theo từng lần phát sinh đối với thu nhập từ chuyển nhượng vốn góp do cá nhân trực tiếp khai thuế và tổ chức, cá nhân khai thuế thay, nộp thuế thay cho cá nhân bao gồm:

(i) Personal income tax declaration form 04/CNV-TNCN (File to download in section 1).

(ii) Appendix of detailed list of individuals transferring capital (applicable to organizations declaring and paying taxes on behalf of many individuals) according to form 04-1/CNV-TNCN issued with [Circular 80/2021/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Thong-tu-80-2021-TT-BTC-huong-dan-Luat-Quan-ly-thue-Nghi-dinh-126-2020-ND-CP-466716.aspx).

(iii) Copy of capital transfer contract. In case of capital contribution by capital contribution, the Capital Transfer Contract is replaced by a copy of the Capital Contribution Contract.

(iv) A copy of the document determining the value of capital contribution according to accounting books. In case of capital contribution due to repurchase, there must be a transfer contract upon purchase.

(v) Copies of documents proving expenses related to determining income from capital contribution transfer activities.

## 3\. Personal income tax declaration dossier for income from securities transfer directly declared by individuals

Personal income tax declaration dossiers for each occurrence of income from securities transfer directly declared by individuals and organizations and individuals declaring and paying taxes on behalf of individuals according to the provisions of Section 9.5 of Appendix I issued with [Decree 126/2020/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Nghi-dinh-126-2020-ND-CP-huong-dan-Luat-Quan-ly-thue-455733.aspx) include:

(i) Personal income tax declaration form 04/CNV-TNCN (File to download in section 1).

(ii) Appendix of detailed list of individuals transferring capital (applicable to organizations declaring and paying taxes on behalf of many individuals) according to form 04-1/CNV-TNCN issued with [Circular 80/2021/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Thong-tu-80-2021-TT-BTC-huong-dan-Luat-Quan-ly-thue-Nghi-dinh-126-2020-ND-CP-466716.aspx).

(iii) Copy of Securities Transfer Contract. In case of capital contribution by securities, the Securities Transfer Contract is replaced by a copy of the Capital Contribution Contract.
