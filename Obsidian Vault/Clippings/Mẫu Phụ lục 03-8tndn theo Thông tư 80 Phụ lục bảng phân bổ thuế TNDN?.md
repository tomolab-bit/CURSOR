## In which cases is corporate income tax allocated?

Pursuant to Clause 1, Article 17 [of Circular 80/2021/TT-BTC,](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/documents/law.aspx?id=I=RZMk56RTTk&mode=09dsbGRWOHhOdzWk) the cases of corporate income tax allocation are stipulated as follows:

\- Electronic lottery business activities.

\- Real estate transfer activities.

\- Dependent unit, business location is production facility.

\- Hydroelectric plants are located in many provinces.

## What is the method of corporate income tax allocation?

According to the provisions of Clause 2, Article 17 [of Circular 80/2021/TT-BTC](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/documents/law.aspx?id=I=RZMk56RTTk&mode=09dsbGRWOHhOdzWk), the method of allocating corporate income tax is as follows:

**\[1\]** Allocation of corporate income tax payable for computerized lottery business activities:

The amount of corporate income tax payable for each province where there is a computerized lottery business = The amount of corporate income tax payable for computerized lottery business x The ratio (%) of actual ticket sales revenue from computerized lottery business in each province to the total actual ticket sales revenue of the taxpayer.

Actual ticket sales revenue from computerized lottery business is determined as follows:

\- In case of the method of distributing computerized lottery tickets through terminals: Revenue from computerized lottery business activities arising from terminals registered to sell computerized lottery tickets within the administrative boundaries of each province according to the lottery agency contract signed with the computerized lottery company or the stores and ticket sales points established by the taxpayer in the area.

\- In case of distributing lottery tickets via phone and internet: Revenue is determined in each province where customers register to participate in the lottery when opening a lottery account according to the provisions of law on computer lottery business.

**\[2\]** Allocation of corporate income tax payable on real estate transfer activities:

The amount of corporate income tax payable for each province where there is real estate transfer activity is provisionally paid quarterly and finalized = Revenue for calculating corporate income tax of real estate transfer activity in each province multiplied by 1%.

**\[3\]** Allocation of corporate income tax payable for taxpayers with dependent units and business locations that are production establishments:

The amount of corporate income tax payable in each province where the production facility is located = The amount of corporate income tax payable for production and business activities multiplied by the ratio (%) of the cost of each production facility to the total cost of the taxpayer (excluding the cost of activities enjoying corporate income tax incentives). The cost to determine the allocation ratio is the actual cost incurred in the tax period.

The amount of corporate income tax payable for production and business activities does not include the amount of corporate income tax payable for activities enjoying corporate income tax incentives. The amount of corporate income tax payable for activities enjoying incentives is determined based on the production and business results of the activities enjoying incentives and the level of incentives enjoyed.

**\[4\]** Allocation of corporate income tax payable for hydropower plants located in many provinces:

The amount of corporate income tax payable by a hydroelectric plant = The amount of corporate income tax payable by production and business activities multiplied by the ratio (%) of the cost of each hydroelectric plant to the total cost of the taxpayer (excluding the cost of activities enjoying corporate income tax incentives). The cost to determine the allocation ratio is the actual cost incurred in the tax period. The amount of corporate income tax payable by production and business activities does not include the amount of corporate income tax payable for activities enjoying corporate income tax incentives.

After determining the amount of corporate income tax payable of the hydroelectric plant, the amount of corporate income tax payable for each province = Amount of corporate income tax payable of the hydroelectric plant multiplied by the ratio (%) of the investment value of the part of the hydroelectric plant located within the administrative boundary of each province to the total investment value of the hydroelectric plant.
