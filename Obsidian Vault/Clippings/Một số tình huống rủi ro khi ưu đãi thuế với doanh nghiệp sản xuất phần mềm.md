## Tax incentives for software production

**Tax incentives for the Software sector** according to the guidelines, software production enterprises enjoy very high incentives including:

- **Preferential tax rate** applies: (i) 10% within 15 years from the date of revenue generation
- Tax reduction **:** (ii) 4 years of exemption (100% reduction) and 9 years of reduction (50%) of tax payable from the time of taxable income

The tax exemption and reduction period is calculated continuously from the first year the enterprise has taxable income from the new investment project that enjoys tax incentives. In case the enterprise has no taxable income in the first three years, from the first year of revenue from the new investment project, the tax exemption and reduction period is calculated from the fourth year the new investment project generates revenue.

**Value Added Tax**

Software products are not subject to VAT but are subject to 0% VAT when exported. Therefore, software exporting enterprises will be refunded input VAT when exporting software products.
