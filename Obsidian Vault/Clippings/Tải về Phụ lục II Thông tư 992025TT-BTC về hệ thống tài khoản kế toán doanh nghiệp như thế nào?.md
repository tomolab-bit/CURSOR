## What are the regulations on accounting accounts according to Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC?

According to Article 11 [of Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC,](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Thong-tu-99-2025-TT-BTC-huong-dan-Che-do-ke-toan-doanh-nghiep-565484.aspx) regulations on accounting accounts are as follows:

(1) Enterprises apply the accounting system in Appendix II issued with [Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Thong-tu-99-2025-TT-BTC-huong-dan-Che-do-ke-toan-doanh-nghiep-565484.aspx) to serve the accounting recording of economic transactions arising at the enterprise.

(2) In order to conform to the characteristics of production and business activities and management requirements of the unit, the enterprise may amend or supplement the name, number, structure and reflection content of the accounting accounts as guided in Appendix II issued with [Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Thong-tu-99-2025-TT-BTC-huong-dan-Che-do-ke-toan-doanh-nghiep-565484.aspx).

The amendments and supplements must ensure the classification and systematization of arising transactions according to economic content, without duplication of subjects, compliance with prescribed accounting principles and must not change or affect the indicators and information presented in the Financial Statements.

When amending or supplementing the names, numbers, structures and contents of accounting accounts, enterprises are responsible for issuing accounting regulations (or equivalent documents) on the amended or supplemented contents to serve as a basis for implementation. The regulations must clearly state the necessity of such amendments or supplements and the enterprise's responsibility before the law for the amended or supplemented contents.

In case the enterprise does not modify or supplement the name, number, structure and reflection content of the accounting accounts, it shall apply the accounting account system guided in Appendix II issued with [Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Thong-tu-99-2025-TT-BTC-huong-dan-Che-do-ke-toan-doanh-nghiep-565484.aspx).

(3) [Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Thong-tu-99-2025-TT-BTC-huong-dan-Che-do-ke-toan-doanh-nghiep-565484.aspx) only provides guidance on the content and methods of accounting for some major economic transactions. In case an enterprise has economic transactions that have not been guided on accounting in this Circular, the enterprise shall base on the content and nature of the arising economic transactions, the provisions of [the 2015 Accounting Law](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Ke-toan-Kiem-toan/Luat-ke-toan-2015-298369.aspx), the guiding documents of [the 2015 Accounting Law](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Ke-toan-Kiem-toan/Luat-ke-toan-2015-298369.aspx), Vietnamese Accounting Standards and the guiding principles in [Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Thong-tu-99-2025-TT-BTC-huong-dan-Che-do-ke-toan-doanh-nghiep-565484.aspx) to implement.

## What principles do businesses apply when changing accounting policies?

According to Clause 1, Article 30 [of Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC,](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Doanh-nghiep/Thong-tu-99-2025-TT-BTC-huong-dan-Che-do-ke-toan-doanh-nghiep-565484.aspx) enterprises must apply the following principles when there is a change in accounting policy:

(1) In case an enterprise has to change its accounting policy due to the first application of legal provisions or Vietnamese Accounting Standards, an accounting regime that has specific conversion instructions (applying retroactive, simple retroactive or non-retroactive adjustment methods), it must follow those instructions. In which:

\- Retroactive or non-retroactive adjustment method is implemented according to the provisions of Vietnamese Accounting Standard No. 29 - Changes in accounting policies, accounting estimates and errors.

\- The simple retrospective adjustment method is a method that does not restate comparative figures from the first affected period but calculates the cumulative impact at the first day of the accounting period in which the new accounting policy is first applied and adjusts the corresponding asset and liability items to undistributed earnings after tax or other items in equity at the first day of the accounting period in which the new accounting policy is applied.

(2) In case an enterprise has to change its accounting policy due to the first application of legal provisions or Vietnamese Accounting Standards, an accounting regime that does not require retroactive or simple retroactive adjustment, the method of non-retroactive adjustment of that accounting policy may be applied.

(3) In case an enterprise voluntarily changes its accounting policy, it must apply the change in accounting policy retrospectively.
