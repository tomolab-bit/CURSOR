**This is a regulation in Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC guiding the Enterprise Accounting Regime, issued on October 27, 2025.**

Article 11 [of Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC](https://luatvietnam.vn/doanh-nghiep/thong-tu-99-2025-tt-btc-huong-dan-che-do-ke-toan-doanh-nghiep-tu-01-01-2026-417085-d1.html) stipulates the accounting account system. Accordingly, enterprises apply the accounting account system in Appendix II issued with Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC to serve the accounting recording of economic transactions arising at the enterprise.

In order to suit the characteristics of production and business activities and management requirements of the unit, the enterprise may amend and supplement the name, number, structure and reflection content of the accounting accounts as guided in Appendix II issued with Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC.

![From January 1, 2026, enterprises shall issue regulations on opening accounting accounts.](https://luatvietnam.vn/tin-van-ban-moi/)

From January 1, 2026, enterprises will issue regulations on opening accounting accounts (Illustration photo)

Amendments **and supplements must ensure the classification and systematization of arising transactions according to economic content, no duplication of objects,** compliance with prescribed accounting principles and must not change or affect the indicators and information presented in the Financial Statements.

**When amending or supplementing the names, numbers, structures and contents of accounting accounts, enterprises are responsible for issuing regulations on opening accounts** (or equivalent documents) on the amended or supplemented contents to serve as a basis for implementation. The regulations must **clearly state the necessity of the amendment or supplement and the responsibility of the enterprise before the law** for the amended or supplemented contents.

In case the enterprise does not modify or supplement the name, number, structure and reflection content of the accounting accounts, it shall apply the accounting account system guided in Appendix II issued with Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC.

Circular 99/2025/TT-BTC only provides guidance on the content and methods of accounting for a number of economic transactions that have incurred capital expenditures.

In case an enterprise has economic transactions that have not been guided on accounting in this Circular, the enterprise shall base on the content and nature of the economic transactions, the provisions of the Law on Accounting, documents guiding the Law on Accounting, Vietnamese Accounting Standards and the guiding principles in Circular 200/2014/TT-BTC for implementation.

*Thus, compared with Clause 1, Article 9 [of Circular 200/2014/TT-BTC](https://luatvietnam.vn/doanh-nghiep/thong-tu-200-2014-tt-btc-huong-dan-che-do-ke-toan-doanh-nghiep-92289-d1.html) on the Accounting Account System, the new regulation*

*Eliminate the mechanism of "asking permission from the Ministry of Finance", switch to giving autonomy to businesses in opening, editing, and supplementing accounting systems.*

*In addition, businesses are required to issue regulations on opening accounts and be responsible before the law.*

*At the same time, maintain the consistency of financial reports, not allowing changes that affect reporting indicators.*
