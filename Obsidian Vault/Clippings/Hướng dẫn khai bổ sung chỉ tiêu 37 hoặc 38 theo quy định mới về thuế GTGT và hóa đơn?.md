## Instructions for additional declaration of index 37 or 38 according to new regulations on VAT and invoices?

***\>> See more [Instructions on adjusting input VAT reduction on VAT declaration due to late payment under contract according to Official Dispatch 1744?](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/ma-so-thue/phap-luat-thue/huong-dan-dieu-chinh-giam-thue-gtgt-dau-vao-tren-to-khai-thue-gtgt-do-thanh-toan-cham-theo-hop-dong-974824-212982.html)***

Pursuant to Point d, Clause 1, Article 14 [of the Law on Value Added Tax 2024](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Luat-Thue-gia-tri-gia-tang-2024-so-48-2024-QH15-556390.aspx?anchor=dieu_14), taxpayers shall make additional declarations in the month or quarter in which the input value added tax amount is incorrect, if the tax declaration in the month or quarter in which the additional value added tax payable increases or the corresponding refunded tax amount is recovered and late payment fees are paid to the state budget (if any).

Taxpayers who make monthly or quarterly declarations discover errors or omissions if the tax declaration in the month or quarter in which the value added tax arises is incorrect or omitted, resulting in a reduction in the amount of tax payable or only increasing or decreasing the amount of value added tax that can be deducted and carried forward to the following month or quarter.

According to Article 19 [of Decree 123/2020/ND-CP,](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Ke-toan-Kiem-toan/Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-quy-dinh-hoa-don-chung-tu-445980.aspx?anchor=dieu_19) amended by Clause 13, Article 1 [of Decree 70/2025/ND-CP,](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Nghi-dinh-70-2025-ND-CP-sua-doi-Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-hoa-don-chung-tu-577816.aspx?anchor=khoan_13_1) in case of recording adjustment invoices or replacement invoices specified in Point b, Clause 1, Article 19 [of Decree 123/2020/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Ke-toan-Kiem-toan/Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-quy-dinh-hoa-don-chung-tu-445980.aspx?anchor=dieu_19), the seller or buyer must make additional declarations in the period in which the adjusted or replaced invoice arises.

Thus, for example, in the case of writing an incorrect invoice, the seller issues an adjustment invoice leading to an increase in the buyer's deductible input VAT, the buyer will declare it additionally in the adjusted invoice period according to [Decree 123/2020/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Ke-toan-Kiem-toan/Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-quy-dinh-hoa-don-chung-tu-445980.aspx) amending and supplementing [Decree 70/2025/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Nghi-dinh-70-2025-ND-CP-sua-doi-Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-hoa-don-chung-tu-577816.aspx) or declare it in the adjustment period according to [the Law on Value Added Tax 2024](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Luat-Thue-gia-tri-gia-tang-2024-so-48-2024-QH15-556390.aspx).

**Below is a specific case with instructions for declaring additional indicators 37 or 38 according to the new regulations on VAT and invoices for easy visualization:**

Currently, according to the provisions of [the Law on Value Added Tax 2024](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Luat-Thue-gia-tri-gia-tang-2024-so-48-2024-QH15-556390.aspx) regarding incorrect or missing declaration of input VAT, it is not the declaration of input VAT of adjustment or replacement invoices.

In addition, pursuant to Clause 5, Article 19 [of Decree 123/2020/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Ke-toan-Kiem-toan/Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-quy-dinh-hoa-don-chung-tu-445980.aspx?anchor=dieu_19), amended by Clause 13, Article 1 [of Decree 70/2025/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Nghi-dinh-70-2025-ND-CP-sua-doi-Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-hoa-don-chung-tu-577816.aspx?anchor=khoan_13_1) applicable to adjustment and replacement invoices:

\- Adjusted invoices and replacement invoices in the cases specified in Point b, Clause 1, Article 19 [of Decree 123/2020/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Ke-toan-Kiem-toan/Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-quy-dinh-hoa-don-chung-tu-445980.aspx?anchor=dieu_19), the seller and buyer shall make additional declarations in the period in which the adjusted or replaced invoice arises;

\- Adjustment invoices for the cases specified in Clause 4, Article 19 [of Decree 123/2020/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Ke-toan-Kiem-toan/Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-quy-dinh-hoa-don-chung-tu-445980.aspx?anchor=dieu_19), the seller shall declare in the period in which the adjustment invoice arises, and the buyer shall declare in the period in which the adjustment invoice is received.

**\[1\] For sellers**

\- In case the seller writes an incorrect invoice, then adjusts the invoice according to the provisions of Clause 1, Article 19 [of Decree 123/2020/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Ke-toan-Kiem-toan/Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-quy-dinh-hoa-don-chung-tu-445980.aspx?anchor=dieu_19) amended by Clause 13, Article 1 [of Decree 70/2025/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Nghi-dinh-70-2025-ND-CP-sua-doi-Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-hoa-don-chung-tu-577816.aspx?anchor=khoan_13_1), the adjustment increases the VAT amount of goods and services sold on the invoice, the seller shall declare an additional amount in the period in which the adjusted or replaced invoice arises (original period) => according to the provisions of [Decree 123/2020/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Ke-toan-Kiem-toan/Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-quy-dinh-hoa-don-chung-tu-445980.aspx) amended and supplemented by [Decree 70/2025/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Nghi-dinh-70-2025-ND-CP-sua-doi-Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-hoa-don-chung-tu-577816.aspx)

\- If the additional declaration increases the tax payable, the seller must pay the increased difference and late payment fee to the state budget. If the additional declaration only reduces the deductible tax amount, the seller must simultaneously declare a reduction in that deduction in indicator 37 of the period in which the adjusted invoice arises (current period) => according to Article 14 [of the Law on Value Added Tax 2024](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Luat-Thue-gia-tri-gia-tang-2024-so-48-2024-QH15-556390.aspx?anchor=dieu_14)

**\[2\] For buyers**

\- The buyer declares an additional increase in the deductible VAT amount and the invoice period is adjusted or replaced (original period) according to the provisions of [Decree 123/2020/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Ke-toan-Kiem-toan/Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-quy-dinh-hoa-don-chung-tu-445980.aspx) amended and supplemented by [Decree 70/2025/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Nghi-dinh-70-2025-ND-CP-sua-doi-Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-hoa-don-chung-tu-577816.aspx)

\- If the additional declaration of the original period reduces the amount of tax payable or increases the amount of deductible input tax, the buyer shall declare it in the current period's indicator 38 according to [the Law on Value Added Tax 2024.](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Luat-Thue-gia-tri-gia-tang-2024-so-48-2024-QH15-556390.aspx)

\- Input invoices have been created, without errors, without adjustments or replacement of invoices but have not been declared, leading to omission of invoices, only additional declarations must be made according to [the Law on Value Added Tax 2024.](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Luat-Thue-gia-tri-gia-tang-2024-so-48-2024-QH15-556390.aspx) Additional declarations must be made to the current period's 38 indicators due to increasing the deductible tax amount or reducing the tax payable in the original period.

**For example,** [Decree 181/2025/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Nghi-dinh-181-2025-ND-CP-huong-dan-Luat-Thue-gia-tri-gia-tang-646124.aspx) guiding [the Law on Value Added Tax 2024](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Luat-Thue-gia-tri-gia-tang-2024-so-48-2024-QH15-556390.aspx) stipulates the adjustment of value added tax in the case of "taxpayers making monthly or quarterly declarations and discovering errors or omissions if the tax declaration in the month or quarter in which the input value added tax arises is incorrect or omitted, reducing the amount of tax payable or only increasing or decreasing the amount of value added tax that can be deducted and carried forward to the following month or quarter.

**\=> In this case, the taxpayer will declare additional tax declaration documents according to regulations and declare in index 37 or 38 of the current period, but not declare input VAT deductible in the period.**

## Có được khấu trừ thuế GTGT nếu sau thời hạn thanh toán mà có chứng từ thanh toán không dùng tiền mặt không?

Căn cứ theo khoản 2 Điều 14 [Luật Thuế giá trị gia tăng 2024](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Luat-Thue-gia-tri-gia-tang-2024-so-48-2024-QH15-556390.aspx?anchor=dieu_14) quy định về điều kiện khấu trừ thuế giá trị gia tăng đầu vào được quy định như sau:

**\[1\]** Có hóa đơn giá trị gia tăng mua hàng hóa, dịch vụ hoặc chứng từ nộp thuế giá trị gia tăng ở khâu nhập khẩu hoặc chứng từ nộp thuế giá trị gia tăng thay cho phía nước ngoài quy định tại khoản 3 và khoản 4 Điều 4 [Luật Thuế giá trị gia tăng 2024](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Luat-Thue-gia-tri-gia-tang-2024-so-48-2024-QH15-556390.aspx?anchor=dieu_4). Bộ trưởng Bộ Tài chính quy định chứng từ nộp thuế giá trị gia tăng thay cho phía nước ngoài;

**\[2\]** Có chứng từ thanh toán không dùng tiền mặt đối với hàng hóa, dịch vụ mua vào, trừ một số trường hợp đặc thù theo quy định của Chính phủ;

**\[3\]** Đối với hàng hóa, dịch vụ xuất khẩu, ngoài các điều kiện quy định tại Mục **\[1\]** và **\[2\]** còn phải có: hợp đồng ký kết với bên nước ngoài về việc bán, gia công hàng hóa, cung cấp dịch vụ; hóa đơn bán hàng hóa, cung cấp dịch vụ; chứng từ thanh toán không dùng tiền mặt; tờ khai hải quan đối với hàng hóa xuất khẩu; phiếu đóng gói, vận đơn, chứng từ bảo hiểm hàng hóa (nếu có). Chính phủ quy định về điều kiện khấu trừ đối với trường hợp xuất khẩu hàng hóa qua sàn thương mại điện tử ở nước ngoài và một số trường hợp đặc thù khác.

Theo đó, một trong những điều kiện để được khấu trừ thuế GTGT là phải có chứng từ thanh toán không dùng tiền mặt đối với hàng hóa, dịch vụ mua vào, trừ một số trường hợp đặc thù theo quy định của Chính phủ.

Đồng thời, Thuế tỉnh Vĩnh Long đã ban hành Công văn 434/VLO-QLDN2 ( [Tải về](https://cdn.thuvienphapluat.vn/uploads/mst/images/MyDuyen/434-VLO-QLDN2.pdf)) hướng dẫn về việc chậm thanh toán theo hợp đồng hoặc phụ lục hợp đồng.

Theo đó, đối với hàng hóa, dịch vụ mua trả chậm, trả góp có giá trị hàng hóa, dịch vụ mua từ 5 (năm) triệu đồng trở lên, mà đến thời điểm thanh toán theo hợp đồng hoặc phụ lục hợp đồng, cơ sở kinh doanh không có chứng từ thanh toán không dùng tiền mặt thì cơ sở kinh doanh phải kê khai, điều chỉnh giảm số thuế GTGT đầu vào được khấu trừ đối với phần giá trị hàng hóa, dịch vụ không có chứng từ thanh toán không dùng tiền mặt vào kỳ tính thuế phát sinh nghĩa vụ thanh toán theo hợp đồng hoặc phụ lục hợp đồng.

Nếu là hàng hóa, dịch vụ mua trả chậm đến thời điểm thanh toán theo hợp đồng hoặc phụ lục hợp đồng Công ty không có chứng từ thanh toán và Công ty đã kê khai điều chỉnh giảm thuế GTGT đầu vào đã khấu trừ nhưng sau đó (sau thời hạn thỏa thuận trả chậm theo hợp đồng hoặc phụ lục hợp đồng) Công ty có chứng từ thanh toán không dùng tiền mặt thì trường hợp này cũng không được kê khai khấu trừ thuề GTGT đầu vào.

Dó đó, nếu sau thời hạn thanh toán mà chứng có từ thanh toán không dùng tiền mặt thì cũng không được khấu trừ thuế GTGT đầu vào.

## Hóa đơn bán hàng có kê khai thuế GTGT không?

Firstly, at Clause 2, Article 8 [of Decree 123/2020/ND-](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Ke-toan-Kiem-toan/Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-quy-dinh-hoa-don-chung-tu-445980.aspx?anchor=dieu_8) CP, amended by Point b, Clause 5, Article 1 [of Decree 70/2025/ND-CP,](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Nghi-dinh-70-2025-ND-CP-sua-doi-Nghi-dinh-123-2020-ND-CP-hoa-don-chung-tu-577816.aspx?anchor=diem_b_5_1) the types of invoices are regulated as follows:

> ***Invoice type***

> *Invoices prescribed in this Decree include the following types:*

> *...*

> *2\. Sales invoice is an invoice for the following organizations and individuals:*

> *a)* ***Organizations and individuals declare and calculate value added tax using the direct method*** *for the following activities:*

> *\- Selling goods and providing services domestically;*

> *\- International transport activities;*

> *\- Export to duty-free zones and cases considered as export;*

> *\- Exporting goods and providing services abroad*

> *b) Organizations and individuals in duty-free zones, when selling goods and providing services domestically and when selling goods and providing services between organizations and individuals in duty-free zones, exporting goods and providing services abroad, must clearly state on the invoice "For organizations and individuals in duty-free zones".*

> *Export processing enterprises with other business activities (other than export processing activities as prescribed by law on industrial parks and export processing zones) declaring value added tax by the direct method shall use invoices according to point a of this clause. Export processing enterprises declaring value added tax by the deduction method shall use invoices according to the provisions of clause 1 of this Article.*

> *...*

Accordingly, it can be seen that sales invoices are invoices for organizations and individuals to declare and **calculate value added tax using the direct method.**

In addition, pursuant to Article 25 [of Decree 181/2025/ND-CP](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/van-ban/Thue-Phi-Le-Phi/Nghi-dinh-181-2025-ND-CP-huong-dan-Luat-Thue-gia-tri-gia-tang-646124.aspx?anchor=dieu_25), regulations on invoices and tax payment documents subject to conditions for input VAT deduction are specifically as follows:

> ***Invoices, tax payment documents***

> *Business establishments must have value-added invoices for purchased goods and services or documents for payment of value-added tax at the import stage or documents for payment of value-added tax on behalf of foreign parties as prescribed in Point a, Clause 2, Article 14 of the Law on Value-Added Tax (including documents for payment of value-added tax at a percentage rate multiplied by revenue on behalf of foreign parties).*

Accordingly, to deduct input VAT, there must be **a value added tax invoice.**

Thus, in case of non-VAT sales invoices to deduct input VAT, there must be a value added tax invoice.

At the same time, sales invoices (direct invoices) are not VAT invoices, so they are not declared in the VAT declaration and VAT cannot be deducted for input invoices.
